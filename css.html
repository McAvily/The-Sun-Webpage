Clown Pierce

ClownPierce (born: March 19, 2002 [age 20]) is a Dutch YouTuber and streamer. He is known for (according to ParrotX2) being one of the best PvP players in Minecraft with a YouTube presence. He is also a member of the LifeSteal SMP, a Content Creator Minecraft server with a gimmick, which lets players steal each other's hearts. He is currently dating a 19 yearold woman by the username of PaigeThePigeon, who doesn't have much of an online presence. He streams semi-often on Twitch and once or twice a month uploads on YouTube. He also occasionally streams other games like Elden Ring.
